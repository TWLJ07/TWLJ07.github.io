Mechanical work

Autonomous drone-glider deployment system

Designed and prototyped an autonomous drone-glider system capable of transporting and deploying drones from within its hull. The glider featured fixed wings, an electric propulsion system, and a pressure-rated end cap equipped with a pressure relief valve (PRV) and external cable interfaces. Internally, the system integrated a battery pack, Wi-Fi communications PCB, and power distribution PCB to support onboard operations. A custom drone deployment mechanism was developed, consisting of an actuator-driven transfer plate that positions drones onto an elevator system, which then raises them out of the hull for launch.
